## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created cause of the needs of people with AuDHD, cause other apps don't consider this needs, leaving this people with no other option than adapt to the specific app, Focus Bear tries to tackle this issue by implementing AuDHD friendly features and design, without leaving user with no AuDHD behind.
## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
Mainly it solves productivity and habit adherence (executive functions) problems
## Why do you think this mission is important?
Is crucial to tackle this issues, cause it can cause a mental and physical toll for people in the long run, it can affect their work and personal life, it also can lead to a tragic outcome because of the stigma that one can have if they have problems with productivity.
